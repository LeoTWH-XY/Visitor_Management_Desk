JaGaApp Integrated Home Screen Rebrand

Preserving all legacy property cards while consolidating a smart, interactive Upcoming Visitors Hub.

REVISED UX CHANGES

1. High-Visibility History CTA Link

Instead of occupying excessive space at the bottom of the feed, the Visitor History Log has been relocated to a quick-routing CTA link within the header of the Visitor Desk. Clicking it triggers a smooth bottom-sheet ledger with filters.

2. Upcoming Visitors Aggregation Card

Today's visitor slot has evolved into a smart "Upcoming" container. It showcases the immediate next scheduled arrival, and includes an aggregated counter (e.g., "+2 more") for other registered guests.

3. Tap-to-Manage Sheet

Tapping the Upcoming Card slides up a beautiful, dedicated overview where residents can see specific arrival calendars, cancel a scheduled entrance, or quickly share access codes to messaging channels.

4. Fully Preserved Original Context

The layout continues to carry all legacy items: outstanding RM billing, cover photo, guard hotline shortcuts, and the structured utilities grid at the bottom.

PROTOTYPE INSTRUCTIONS

Use the simulated mobile screen on the right. Try creating an Upcoming/Overnight guest with the quick action forms, click the Upcoming card to inspect the guest lists, or click the History link at the top of the Desk to pull up past logs.
